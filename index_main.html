Welcome to Technoblitz 2013 official site!
A bit about Technoblitz:

TECHNOBLITZ is an event organized by Department of Engineering Sciences and Humanities of Marathwada Institute of Technology, Aurangabad. This event is specifically organized for the first year students of all the engineering colleges in Aurangabad city. The purpose behind organizing this event is to provide a platform for the students of first year to showcase their talent and expertise.

Venue: MIT, Aurangabad

2nd and 3rd March 2013

All events are open for first year engineering students. Those who are intersted to participate contact respective organizers. All the events will be conducted as per the schedule.

...Come, innovate yourself!!!
